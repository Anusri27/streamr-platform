### RandomNumber

Generate random numbers between [*min*, *max*] with uniform probability.

**Inputs**
**trigger**
when value is received, activates module


**Outputs**
**out**
the random number


**Parameters**
**min**
lower bound of interval to sample from
**max**
upper bound of interval to sample from
