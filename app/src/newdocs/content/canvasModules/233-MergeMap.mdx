### MergeMap

Merge **rightMap** onto **leftMap** resulting in a single map. In case of conflicting keys, entries of **rightMap** will replace those of **leftMap**.

**Inputs**
**leftMap**
a map to merge onto
**rightMap**
a map to be merged


**Outputs**
**out**
the resulting merged map
