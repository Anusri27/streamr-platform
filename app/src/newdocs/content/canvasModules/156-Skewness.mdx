### Skewness

Calculates the skewness (or third standardized moment) of a distribution of values in a sliding window. Skewness is a measure of the asymmetry of a distribution.

**Inputs**
**in**
Input random variable


**Outputs**
**out**
Skewness


**Parameters**
**minSamples**
Number of samples required to produce output
**windowLength**
Length of the sliding window of values
