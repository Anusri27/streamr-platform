### MovingAverageExp

Smooths the incoming time series by calculating an exponential moving average (EMA)  





	- <span class="formula">EMA(t) = a x <strong>in</strong>(t) + (1-a) x EMA(t-1)</span>
	- <span class="formula">a = <span class="math-tex">\(2 \over \text{length} + 1\)</span></span>
