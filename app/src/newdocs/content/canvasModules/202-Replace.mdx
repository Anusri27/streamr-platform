### Replace

Searches the input text by the **search **parameter, and if it is found, replaces it with the **replaceWith **parameter and outputs the result.




**Outputs**
**out**
The output, with replaced texts


**Parameters**
**search**
The substring to be replaced
**replaceWith**
The replacer
