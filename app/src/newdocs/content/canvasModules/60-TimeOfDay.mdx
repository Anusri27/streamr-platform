### TimeOfDay

Outputs 1 if the current time of day (in the time zone your user account is set to) is between <span class="highlight">startTime</span> and <span class="highlight">endTime</span> (both inclusive). At other times outputs 0.




**Outputs**
**out**
1 between the given times, otherwise 0


**Parameters**
**startTime**
24 hour format HH:MM:SS
**endTime**
24 hour format HH:MM:SS
