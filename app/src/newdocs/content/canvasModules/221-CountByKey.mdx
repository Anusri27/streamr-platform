### CountByKey

Keeps count of the occurrences of keys.

**Inputs**
**key**
The (string) key


**Outputs**
**map**
Key-count pairs
**valueOfCurrentKey**
The occurrence count of the last key received. 


**Parameters**
**sort**
Whether key-count pairs should be order by count
**maxKeyCount**
Maximum number of (sorted) key-count pairs to keep. Everything else will be dropped.
