### LinearMapper

Linearly transforms a range of incoming (x) values to a range of outgoing (y) values. For example, this could be used to transform the input range of -1...1 (<span class="highlight">xMin</span>...<span class="highlight">xMax</span>) into an output range of 0...1000 (<span class="highlight">yMin</span>...<span class="highlight">yMax</span>).
  


Incoming values outside the x range will just output the min/max y value.
