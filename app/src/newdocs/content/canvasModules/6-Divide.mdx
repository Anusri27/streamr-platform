### Divide

This module calculates the quotient of its two input values. If the input <span class="highlight">B</span> is zero, the result is not defined and thus no output is produced.

**Inputs**
**A**
The dividend, or numerator
**B**
The divisor, or denominator


**Outputs**
**A/B**
The quotient: A divided by B
